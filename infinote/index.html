what is this?

infinote is an interconnected annotation environment designed for tangential thinkers.

Inspired by marginalia and non-linear brainstorming, infinote acknowledges that thoughts naturally diverge, and that a bringing together of ideas (even extraneous ones) can be not only enlightening, but delightful.

The strength of infinote lies in three core ideas:

interconnectivity.
a porous ecosystem.
infinite space.
